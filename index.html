Our Core Mandate

Protecting the Integrity of Private Schools

NAPPS Ogun State Chapter stands as the supreme regulatory body for private educational institutions. We provide the essential framework for curriculum excellence, administrative support, and governmental liaison.

Through this portal, we ensure that every member school is properly vetted, creating a trusted, transparent environment for parents, students, and educators across the Gateway State.
